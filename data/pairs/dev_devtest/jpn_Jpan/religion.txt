400万人以上が葬儀に参列しようとローマに向かいました。	Over four million people went to Rome to attend the funeral.
参列者の数が多すぎて、全員がサンピエトロ広場での葬儀に参加することは不可能でした。	The number of people present was so large that it was not possible for everybody to gain access to the funeral in St. Peter's Square.
ローマの各所には、人々に式典を見てもらおうと、大型テレビ画面がいくつも設置されていました。	Several large television screens were installed in various places in Rome to let the people watch the ceremony.
イタリア国内の他の多くの都市や世界の他の地域（特にポーランド）で同じように設置がされ、多くの人々が視聴しました。	In many other cities of Italy and in the rest of the world, particularly in Poland, similar setups were made, which were viewed by a great number of people.
