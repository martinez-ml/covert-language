En 1884, Tesla se mudó a EE. UU. a fin de tomar un empleo en la compañía Edison en Nueva York.	In 1884, Tesla moved to the United States of America to accept a job with the Edison Company in New York City.
Arribó en los EE. UU. con 4 centavos, un libro de poemas y una nota de recomendación hecha por Charles Batchelor (su gerente en su último empleo) y dirigida a Thomas Edison.	He arrived in the US with 4 cents to his name, a book of poetry, and a letter of recommendation from Charles Batchelor (his manager in his previous job) to Thomas Edison.
