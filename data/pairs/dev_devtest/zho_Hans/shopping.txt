免税购物是指在特定地点购买免税商品的机会。	Duty free shopping is the opportunity to buy goods exempted from taxes and excises at certain locations.
前往课征重税的国家/地区的旅客有时可以省下一大笔钱，在这些地方购买酒水和烟草等产品特别划算。	Travellers bound for countries with heavy taxation can sometimes save a considerable amount of money, especially on products such as alcoholic beverages and tobacco.
