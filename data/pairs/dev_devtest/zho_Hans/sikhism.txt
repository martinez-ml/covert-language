锡克教是来自印度次大陆的一种宗教。它在 15 世纪起源于旁遮普地区，来自印度教传统的内部宗派分裂。	Sikhism is a religion from the Indian sub-continent. It originated in the Punjab region during the 15th century from a sectarian split within the Hindu tradition.
锡克教徒认为，他们信仰的是与印度教不同的一种宗教，尽管他们承认锡克教源自印度教，也包含印度教的传统。	Sikhs consider their faith to be a separate religion from Hinduism though they acknowledge its Hindu roots and traditions.
锡克教徒把自己的宗教称为 Gurmat，在旁遮普语中是“古鲁（导师）之路”的意思。古鲁是所有印度宗教的基本组成部分，但在锡克教中地位特别高，成为了锡克教信仰的核心。	Sikhs call their religion Gurmat, which is Punjabi for "way of the guru". The guru is a fundamental aspect of all Indian religions but in Sikhism has taken on an importance that forms the core of Sikh beliefs.
该宗教于 15 世纪由古鲁·纳纳克 (1469-1539) 创立。随后又有九位古鲁。	The religion was founded in the 15th century by Guru Nanak (1469–1539). There followed in succession a further nine gurus.
