学习制作互动媒体需要常规传统技能，以及在互动课程中掌握的工具（故事板制作、音频和视频编辑、故事情节设计等）。	Learning to create interactive media requires conventional and traditional skills, as well as tools mastered in interactive classes (storyboarding, audio and video editing, story telling, etc.)
交互设计要求你重新评估有关媒体制作的假设，并学会非线性思考。	Interactive design requires that you re-assess your assumptions about media production and learn to think in a non-linear ways.
交互设计要求项目的各个组成部分相互联系，但也可以作为一个独立实体存在。	Interactive design requires that components of a project connect to each other, but also make sense as a separate entity.
