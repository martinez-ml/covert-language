神秘主义追求与终极现实、神性、精神真理或上帝进行交流、认同或达成知觉意识。	Mysticism is the pursuit of communion with, identity with, or conscious awareness of an ultimate reality, divinity, spiritual truth, or God.
信徒们寻求一种对神圣的现实/神性或诸神的直接体验、直觉或者顿悟。	The believer seeks a direct experience, intuition, or insight into divine reality/the deity or dieties.
追随者追求某种生活方式或旨在滋养这些经验的修行。	Followers pursue certain ways of living, or practices that are intended to nurture those experiences.
神秘主义可以与其他形式的宗教信仰和崇拜区分开来，因为它强调对独特意识状态的个人直接体验，特别是那些和平的、有洞察力的、幸福的、甚至是狂喜的意识状态。	Mysticism can be distinguished from other forms of religious belief and worship by its emphasis on the direct personal experience of a unique state of consciousness, particularly those of a peaceful, insightful, blissful, or even ecstatic character.
